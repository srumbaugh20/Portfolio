ABOUT THE PROJECT

For my personal project at DevMountain, I decided to make an eCommerce site for my grandma. For years my grandma had been attempting to sell her watercolor art on etsy with little success. Etsy had become too saturated with other sellers. This site not only demonstrates all the skills I lerned at DevMountain but also will allow my grandma to develop a better internet presence to sell her paintings.


Technologies used: HTML, Bootstrap, Sass, Gulp, AngularJS, Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL
